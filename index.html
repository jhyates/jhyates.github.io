About Me

Who I Am

Spacecraft engineer hailing from Rhode Island. Moving to the Bay Area and excited to meet new people while getting outside to hike, rock climb, and ski. Side projects are key, here I'm dabbling in some personal web design.

What I Do

I'm an incoming GNC engineer at Astranis, where I'll be working to build a new generation of telecom small satellites. Previously, I interned at Astranis and Firefly Aerospace (then-Firefly Space Systems), and led the Michigan eXploration Lab's MARIO CubeSat project. I hold an MSE in aerospace engineering from University of Michigan and a BSE in mechanical and aerospace engineering from Princeton University.

Why Am I Doing This?

This website serves several purposes. First, it's cool to have a website where you're totally in control, and it's been a fun side project in learning a little bit of HTML and CSS. I'm excited to see it evolve over time as my goals for the site shift and my abilities grow. Second, it's a convenient resource to point to when introducing myself to people, or want to show off my work. Third, it's an exercise in removing myself from social media – for both privacy and productivity reasons – that provides some of the things that social media does. This lets me take a step back and be pickier about my online presence. Of course, you can still find me on most common social media platforms.
